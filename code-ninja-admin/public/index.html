Club Introduction

Welcome to Code Ninja!, the ultimate hub for competitive coding enthusiasts at Annasaheb Dange College of Engineering and Technology. We are a group of passionate coders from the AI&DS Department, committed to sharpening our problem-solving skills and excelling in coding competitions.

Whether you're a beginner or an expert, Code Ninja! provides a platform to learn, grow, and showcase your coding prowess.

Our Goals

  • Enhance Coding Skills: Through regular practice sessions, workshops, and hackathons.
  • Compete & Conquer: Participate in national and international coding competitions.
  • Collaborate & Innovate: Work together on challenging problems and innovative projects.
  • Career Development: Gain valuable experience and prepare for a future in tech.

Benefits of Joining

  • Access to exclusive resources and coding challenges.
  • Opportunity to participate in inter-college and national-level competitions.
  • Networking with like-minded peers and industry experts.
  • Certification and recognition for your achievements.
